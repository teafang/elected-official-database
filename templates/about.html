About Open Aisle


Open Aisle shares information about elected officials based on street address. Users input a ZIP code to get information about their representatives at the national, state, and local level. They can access phone numbers, addresses, websites, and social media. We aim to help you stay informed and get engaged!

Questions? Contact us at openaisle@gmail.com
